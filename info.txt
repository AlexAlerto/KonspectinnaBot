What is Konspectinna?
Konspectinna is a bot that allows you to store and conveniently view lecture notes, tutorials and labs of your course.

What does the name at the end of the file name mean?
This is the pseudonym of the person who shared his synopsis. This way you can know whose notes you understand better.
